About Us · the cartographers

A small studio drawing a bigger map of who gets to use AI.

planetAI LLP is a Bengaluru-based studio building practical, ethical AI for the people the revolution keeps skipping. World of AI is our ecosystem — one connected planet of products for India's students, parents, and everyday citizens.

We pair two decades of enterprise AI delivery with the speed of a team that ships in public. Every beacon here was built, deployed, and put in front of real students on our own capital — three live today, three more rising.

Our rule is simple: keep the core free and privacy-first, and charge a fraction of what the market asks — so a family never has to choose between guidance and groceries.
